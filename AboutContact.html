Meet the creator

Hey, I’m Marquesia a multimedia creator working across photo, video, and audio. I love capturing moments, building visual stories, and shaping sound to bring ideas to life. My work focuses on capturing beauty whether it’s found in people, places, or the mind. I’m always experimenting with new techniques and tools to push my creativity, and I’m always looking to learn more, grow, and expand my craft. Whether I'm shooting, editing, or mixing, I aim to make work that feels intentional, immersive, and true to the story. Thanks for checking out my portfolio, feel free to look around, and reach out.
